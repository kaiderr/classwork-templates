Class List Information

Looking for a fun introdcution to painting? Want to add professional-looking pieces to your portfolio? Choose one of our classes and explore the possibilities! Classes are offered for all ages and skill levels.

Sign Ups for May are due by Monday, April 22nd. Classes will begin the following week on Monday, April 29th.

Painting 101

This is a beginner's class to all things related to painting. This class is targeted towards adults with little to no experience with painting. All equipment will be provided, but you are free to bring in your own.

Tues and Thurs, 7:00pm - 8:30pm

Pottery and Clay

Designed for anyone interested in pottery, no matter what experience you may have. We will go over how to design a piece, putting it together, and firing it up in the kiln. This class is also great for experienced artists who want to fire up some test pieces or perfect a design.

Mon and Wed, 6:00pm - 8:00pm

Kid's Corner

For all kids 10 years old and up who are interested in pursuing art as either a hobby or a career. Each week, we will provide space and guidance to kids looking to improve their skills. Every month will be the start of a new project pertaining to 2-D art.

Tues 4:00pm - 6:00pm

Advanced Water Color

Have experience with water color? This class will teach you new ideas and techniques to improve your skills. Each week will encapsulate a different topic or idea.

Sun 3:00pm - 4:30pm

Portrait Design

For any artists, of any age, that are interested in painting and drawing portraits. This class will go over the fundamentals of designing portraits, from basic anatomy to shadows and features of the face.

Thurs 5:00pm - 7:00pm

Photography

This class will go over how to improve your photography skills. Whether you're a photographer for portraits, real estate, events or anything in between, this class will enhance your shots to the next level.

Tues 4:00pm-6:00pm
